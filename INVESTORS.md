# Untapped Market — Investor Opportunities & Monetization Plan

> Strategic fundraising and revenue plan for **Untapped Market** — a PNW-first
> cannabis strain discovery, dispensary finder, and personal library app.
> Differentiated by genetic-lineage data, lab COA rigor, terpene profiles, and
> a consumer-product feel (think Letterboxd for cannabis) versus Leafly and
> Weedmaps, which are dispensary marketing platforms.

---

## 1. Where Untapped Market sits in the market

| | Untapped Market | Leafly | Weedmaps | Dutchie / Jane |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Primary user | Consumer | Consumer (ad-driven for dispensaries) | Dispensary directory | Dispensary (POS / e-com) |
| Core value | Genetics, terpenes, COAs, personal library | Strain encyclopedia + menus | Map + menus | Order infrastructure |
| Strain genetic-lineage depth | Deep (parent/offspring trees, regional phenos) | Light | Minimal | None |
| Lab COA depth | Per-batch COA history, terpene-effect correlation | Snapshot only | Minimal | Pass-through if dispensary supplies |
| Geography | PNW-first, depth over breadth | National | National | National |
| Business model | Consumer subscription + B2B data | Ad / lead-gen | Ad / SaaS for dispensaries | SaaS + transaction fees |

**Pitch in one line:** *"Weedmaps is Yelp for dispensaries. Untapped Market is
Letterboxd for what's actually in the jar — built on genetic data, lab COAs,
and the densest cultivar density in the US (the PNW)."*

**Definition used throughout this doc:**

- **MAU** = unique authenticated user with ≥1 session in the trailing 30
  days. Anonymous web visitors are tracked separately and not counted.
- **Premium price** = $5/month or $55/year (~$55–60 blended ARPU/yr).
- **Pro price** (proposed, see §3.2) = $12/month or $130/year (~$130–144
  blended ARPU/yr).

## 3. Profitability plan — how Untapped Market actually makes money

The current $5/mo Premium tier is a starter. The defensible long-term margin
comes from layering **four** revenue streams on the same data flywheel.

### 3.1 Revenue stream summary

| # | Stream | Stage to launch | Margin profile | LTV character |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Consumer subscription (B2C) | Now (live) | High (~85%) | Mid; churn-sensitive |
| 2 | Dispensary lead-gen / affiliate (CPC/CPL) | Month 3–6 | High | Volume-driven |
| 3 | Dispensary B2B SaaS (analytics + menu widget) | Month 9–12 | Very high | Highest LTV |
| 4 | Data licensing to cultivators / brands / labs | Month 12–18 | Highest | Enterprise-style |

Together these turn the consumer app into a **two-sided data product**:
consumer demand on one side, dispensary/brand supply on the other.

### 3.2 Stream 1 — Consumer subscription (Premium)

**Today (live in app):** **$5/month or $55/year** Premium with smart folders,
alerts, COA export, genetic deep-dives, consumption log.

**Near-term roadmap** (sold as roadmap, not as current capability):

- Live inventory map (V2 — Mapbox integration; placeholder shipped today).
- Native iOS/Android apps (V2 — currently web-first).

**Proposed expansion:**

- **Free** — strain catalog, dispensary finder, basic library, trip reports.
- **Premium $5/mo or $55/yr** *(unchanged)* — current feature set.
- **Pro $12/mo or $130/yr** *(new)* — adds:
  - Full COA history per strain (not just current batch)
  - Terpene-effect correlation reports for your saved library
  - Multi-region tracking (e.g., follow strains in both Seattle and Portland)
  - API key for personal use (data export to Notion, etc.)
  - Early access to new cultivar drops

**Conversion targets (deliberately hedged):**
- Free → Premium: **2–4%** of MAU near-term, with 4–5% achievable once saved-
  strain alerts and consumption-log streaks prove sticky.
- Premium → Pro: 10–15% of Premium users.

**Retention levers:**
- Saved strain alerts (utility hook — high stickiness).
- Consumption log streaks (habit hook).
- Personal taste profile that improves over time (data moat against switching).

### 3.3 Stream 2 — Lead-gen / affiliate from dispensaries

The Weedmaps playbook, but earned, not paid-only.

- **Click-to-menu deep links** — when a user taps "in stock at" on a strain
  detail page, that handoff is monetizable. CPC range $0.50–$2.00.
- **"Reserve at this dispensary"** — affiliate kickback on any pre-order
  completed via partner POS (Dutchie, Jane). Negotiable rev-share, typically
  3–8% of order value.
- **Promoted dispensaries** — dispensaries pay to be listed first within a
  ZIP radius. Must be clearly labeled "Sponsored" — App Store + trust both
  demand it.

**Unit economics (the version reflected in §3.8):**

We model revenue *per MAU per year*, not gross click counts, because most
launch dispensaries will not pay full CPC immediately. The model assumes a
**blended ~$1.25 net revenue per MAU per year** in early years, ramping as
more dispensaries enter paid auctions.

- 25K MAU → ~$25K/yr (Year 1, mostly free pilots, very low fill rate).
- 100K MAU → ~$150K/yr (Year 2, $1.50 / MAU / yr — initial paid auctions).
- 400K MAU → ~$500K/yr (Year 3, $1.25 / MAU / yr — rev-share weighted down
  by promoted-listing volume discounting).

**Gross-claim sanity check:** at 50K MAU, 3 menu-link clicks per MAU per
month produces 150K clicks/month. At a $0.75 average gross CPC that's
$1.35M annualized *gross*; we discount this by ~70% for unsold inventory,
launch-pricing pilots, and dispensary churn to land at the per-MAU figures
above. The pitch should lead with the conservative per-MAU number, not the
gross click headline.

### 3.4 Stream 3 — Dispensary B2B SaaS (the moat)

This is the highest-LTV, hardest-to-displace stream and the one most cannabis
investors will get most excited about.

**Product: Untapped Market for Dispensaries** ($199–$799/mo tiered)

- **Demand dashboard** — what strains are users in *your* trade area saving,
  searching, alert-subscribing to. Heatmap by ZIP. Trend lines.
- **Stockout alerts** — your store appears in users' "back in stock" alerts;
  dashboard shows demand for items you don't carry.
- **Embeddable strain cards** — paste a snippet on the dispensary's own site
  to show terpene + COA + lineage data tied to their menu.
- **Competitor benchmark** — anonymized aggregate view of how your menu's
  genetic diversity / lab transparency compares to neighbors.
- **Customer arrival data** — verified-visit pings (opt-in by users) to prove
  app-driven foot traffic.

**Tiers**
- **Listed** — $199/mo — basic dashboard, demand heatmap, embed widget.
- **Plus** — $399/mo — adds competitor benchmarks, stockout alerts, monthly
  insights report.
- **Pro** — $799/mo — adds CSV / API data export, dedicated CSM, A/B menu
  tests.

**TAM math (PNW only, before national expansion)**

PNW dispensary count: WA ~480 active retail licenses + OR ~750 ≈ **~1,200**.
Blended ACV target: **$400/mo ($4,800/yr)** assuming a Listed-heavy mix
early, shifting toward Plus/Pro as data products mature.

| Penetration | Accounts | MRR | ARR |
|---|---|---|---|
| 10% (conservative) | 120 | $48K | **$576K** |
| 20% (mid) | 240 | $96K | **$1.15M** |
| 30% (aggressive) | 360 | $144K | **$1.73M** |

National expansion (CA + CO + MI + IL alone adds ~3,500 retail licenses)
takes the same penetration math comfortably past **$5M ARR** at 10%.

### 3.5 Stream 4 — Data licensing to cultivators, brands, labs

The flywheel: every Premium user's saved-strain, alert, and consumption log
data (anonymized + aggregated) becomes a panel that nobody else has at the
genetic + terpene level of granularity.

- **Cultivator demand reports** — "Pheno X of GMO is 4× more bookmarked in
  Portland than Seattle this month." Sold quarterly to PNW cultivators
  ($2–10K per report, or $25–60K annual subscription).
- **Brand discovery reports** — emerging genetics by region, sold to MSOs
  and consumer brands ($50K+ annual).
- **Lab partner program** — testing labs pay a flat monthly fee ($99–299)
  for a "Verified by ___ Lab" badge on COA entries that came through their
  lab, driving brand awareness with end consumers. (Note: the PNW lab market
  is small — ~5–8 labs — so this is a brand/partnership line, not a major
  revenue line.)
- **Press / industry reports** — quarterly "State of PNW Cultivars" trend
  PDF — paid sponsorship slots from ancillary brands.

### 3.6 Stream 5 — Adjacencies (later)

- **Telehealth referrals** — partner with Heally, NuggMD, etc. for medical
  card sign-ups; rev-share on *converted* card issuance ($20–60 per
  conversion, not per click).
- **Ancillary commerce affiliate** — storage, vapes, glass, scales,
  smell-proof bags. App Store-safe (no plant-touching). 8–15% commission.
- **Education / events** — paid masterclass video series, ticketed PNW
  cultivator tastings, sponsored "Strain of the Month" tours.
- **White-label** — license the strain detail viewer + COA renderer to
  dispensary websites and POS partners.

### 3.7 What we will *not* monetize (and why)

- **No selling individual user identities** — kills consumer trust in a
  privacy-sensitive vertical, and is App Store / GDPR risk.
- **No direct in-app cart / checkout for cannabis** — Apple App Store policy
  (App Review Guidelines 1.4.3 / 1.5) prohibits cannabis transactions inside
  iOS apps and limits dispensary listings to legal jurisdictions. Discovery
  and deep-link handoff to dispensary web flows only.
- **No paid review placement** — we are the trust layer; this is the
  Letterboxd-not-Yelp rule.

### 3.8 3-year revenue model (illustrative, conservative)

**ARPU assumptions used in this table:**
- Premium blended ARPU: **~$57/yr** (mix of $5/mo monthly and $55/yr annual,
  ~50/50 split; annual checkout typical for engaged consumer apps).
- Pro blended ARPU: **~$140/yr** (mix of $12/mo and $130/yr).
- B2B accounts at blended ACV growing $300 → $400 → $500/mo as Plus/Pro
  tiers gain share.

| | Year 1 | Year 2 | Year 3 |
|---|---|---|---|
| MAU | 25,000 | 100,000 | 400,000 |
| Premium conversion | 3% (750) | 4% (4,000) | 5% (20,000) |
| Pro conversion (of Premium, additive) | 10% (75) | 12% (480) | 15% (3,000) |
| **Subscription ARR** | **~$53K** | **~$295K** | **~$1.56M** |
| Dispensary B2B accounts | 10 @ $300/mo | 50 @ $400/mo | 150 @ $500/mo |
| **B2B SaaS ARR** | **~$36K** | **~$240K** | **~$900K** |
| Affiliate / lead-gen | ~$25K | ~$150K | ~$500K |
| Data licensing | $0 | ~$80K | ~$400K |
| **Total ARR** | **~$114K** | **~$765K** | **~$3.36M** |

Halve these for downside. At Year 3 the gross margin profile (mostly
software + data) supports >70% margins.

> **Subscription ARR derivation (Year 3):** 20,000 Premium × $57 +
> 3,000 Pro × $140 = $1,140,000 + $420,000 ≈ **$1.56M**. Pro is modeled
> as additive on top of Premium for users who want the full COA history
> and multi-region tracking. Year 1 and Year 2 follow the same formula:
> 750 × $57 + 75 × $140 ≈ $53K; 4,000 × $57 + 480 × $140 ≈ $295K. The
> earlier draft's $2.36M assumed Premium at $7/$63; this corrected line
> uses the live $5/$55 Premium price.

## 4. Risk disclosures (be upfront with investors)

- **Federal Schedule I status.** Affects banking, payment processors, paid
  acquisition (Meta, Google ad bans on cannabis content), and App Store
  policies. Mitigation: our app is informational/discovery — we don't
  process cannabis transactions.
- **App Store policy risk.** Apple App Review Guidelines 1.4.3 / 1.5 permit
  cannabis information and dispensary discovery in legal jurisdictions but
  prohibit in-app cannabis commerce. Mitigation: deep-link to dispensary
  partner web flows; no in-app cart.
- **State-by-state regulation.** Each new market adds legal review overhead.
  Mitigation: PNW-first depth; expand after dominance.
- **Incumbents.** Leafly (~$36M revenue 2023, declining from ~$43M in 2022;
  public, distressed) and WM Technology / Weedmaps (~$184M revenue 2023;
  public). Differentiation: consumer-product focus, genetic / COA depth,
  PNW-first content moat. *Verify current quarter figures from each
  company's latest 10-K/10-Q before sending the deck.*
- **Cannabis VC market compression.** Capital availability has shrunk vs.
  2020–2021. Mitigation: capital-efficient plan, real revenue path, lean
  team.
- **Data sourcing.** COA scraping / lab partnerships need formal
  agreements. Mitigation: lab partner program (Stream 4) directly aligns
  incentives.
