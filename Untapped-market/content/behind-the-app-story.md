## PIECE 2 — FACEBOOK PINNED "ABOUT US" POST (~350 words)

Every cannabis app in the country is telling you the same thing. "Relaxed. Happy. Creative." That's not data. That's a horoscope. 🌲

We got tired of it too.

Here's the actual problem: the apps people built their recommendations around stopped updating years ago. Strain databases froze in place while Washington and Oregon kept producing some of the most interesting genetics in North America. Meanwhile, the lab data that actually matters — what's in the flower, what terpenes are dominant, what the COA actually says — was either buried three clicks deep or nonexistent for consumers entirely. You were buying on faith and a vibe.

So we built Untapped Market. Not for the whole country. For here. Washington and Oregon only, because doing one region right matters more than doing fifty regions halfway.

What we actually built: real DNA-level strain data — mother, father, lineage, chemotype classification — so you understand what you're looking at before you buy. Lab-verified Certificates of Analysis attached to strains, so you can see the proof instead of taking someone's word for it. Terpene profiles down to the percentage point, because "earthy and relaxing" tells you nothing and myrcene: 0.82% tells you everything. And a dispensary finder that connects you to real inventory near you, so when you find a strain worth tracking down, you can actually find it.

This isn't a national platform trying to be all things to everyone. Cascadia has been growing exceptional cannabis for decades — volcanic ridge soils, maritime fog patterns, preservation breeders who never stopped working. It has its own identity, its own cultivars, its own character. We built something that reflects that.

Two hundred strains catalogued. Forty-seven dispensaries mapped. Twelve thousand community trip reports. And a lab verification layer that flat out didn't exist before we made it. 🌿

If you're in the PNW and you want to know what you're actually buying — this is where you start.
