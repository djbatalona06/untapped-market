# Untapped Market — Investor Brief
**Date:** May 2026
**Target Launch Market:** Pacific Northwest (WA, OR)

## The Problem
Leafly and Weedmaps are dispensary lead-gen and ad marketplaces. They treat cannabis strains as marketing copy, not data. Consumers are sophisticated and want to know exactly what is in the jar—genetic lineage, verified lab data, and terpene profiles—but no consumer platform provides this level of detail tied directly to local inventory.

## Our Wedge & Why PNW
We are launching exclusively in the Pacific Northwest first.
- **Cultivar Density:** The PNW has the highest density of unique cultivars and craft growers.
- **Consumer Demand:** Consumers here demand lab transparency and terpene data.
- **Network Effects:** A tight geographical launch creates strong, localized network effects between consumers, dispensaries, and cultivators.

## The Product (V1 Live)
We have successfully built the V1 web application with a premium, mobile-first design:
1. **Strain Detail:** Deep genetic lineage trees, terpene profiles, and lab-verified cannabinoid data (COAs).
2. **Dispensary Finder:** Live PNW dispensary mapping to find specific strains near you.
3. **Personal Library:** A Letterboxd-style social loop for consumers to save strains, track sessions, and log trip reports.

## Traction (Pre-Launch)
- **Waitlist:** Strong initial waitlist signups (~70% PNW-based).
- **B2B Pipeline:** Verbal LOIs from local dispensaries for the initial pilot.
- **Partnerships:** Pheno-hunt collaborations and COA data integrations in progress with local cultivators.

## Realistic 6-12 Month Goals
To prove our model and reach sustainable growth, our focus for the next 6-12 months is lean and metric-driven:
1. **Launch iOS/Web MVP (Q3 2026):** Fully deploy the V1 product and achieve 5,000 - 10,000 Monthly Active Users (MAU).
2. **Dispensary Onboarding:** Onboard 15–20 PNW dispensaries on a free pilot to build inventory density.
3. **First B2B Revenue:** Convert 2–5 pilot dispensaries/cultivators into paying customers ($200/mo SaaS tier).
4. **Premium Consumer Tier:** Launch the $5.99/mo consumer subscription and convert 2% of our free MAU.

## Financials & The Ask
**Current Stage:** Bootstrapped / Pre-Revenue.
We are currently operating extremely lean, relying on sweat equity, AI-accelerated development, and minimal infrastructure costs.

**The Ask: $250k - $500k Angel/Pre-Seed Round**
Instead of a massive $1.5M raise out the gate, we are raising a focused Angel tranche to achieve our 12-month milestones.
- **50% Product & Engineering:** Finalizing backend infrastructure (Supabase), real-time inventory sync, and React Native mobile app.
- **30% Local Marketing & Operations:** B2B onboarding in Seattle/Portland and grassroots community events.
- **20% Runway & Legal:** Founder living expenses, compliance, and legal infrastructure for licensing data.
